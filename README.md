# Gold Prices and Geopolitical Risk Analysis
## Project Overview

This project examines the relationship between geopolitical risk and gold prices using daily data. Gold is widely regarded as a safe-haven asset, often attracting capital during periods of heightened political uncertainty. The objective of this study is to empirically test whether increases in geopolitical risk are associated with statistically significant movements in gold returns.

The analysis integrates daily gold spot prices with the Geopolitical Risk (GPR) Index to evaluate correlation strength, lag effects, and volatility behavior during high-risk periods.
